🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Photobooth Kawaii. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

1. Camera Access

Photobooth Kawaii requests access to your device's camera solely to enable the photobooth feature. Your camera feed is processed entirely within your browser and is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external server.

2. Photos & Data

All photos you take are stored locally in your browser session only. When you close the page, photos are gone. Downloaded photos go directly to your device — we never receive copies.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use basic analytics (e.g., page views) that do not identify you personally.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts and Font Awesome for typography and icons. These services may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operations. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

5. Children's Privacy

Our site is designed to be safe and fun for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will note the date of the last update at the top of this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
